## Metrics to Track

### Acquisition
- Unique visitors (goal: 5,000 in week 1)
- Sign-up conversion rate (goal: 10%)
- Traffic sources breakdown

### Activation
- % of signups who log first entry (goal: 60%)
- Time to first entry (goal: <5 minutes)

### Retention
- Day 1 retention (goal: 40%)
- Day 7 retention (goal: 20%)

### Revenue
- Free → Pro conversion rate (goal: 3%)
- MRR growth
- Trial-to-paid conversion

## Risk Mitigation

### If Site Goes Down
1. Check server status and logs
2. Enable maintenance page if needed
3. Post update on social media
4. Have rollback plan ready

### If Negative Feedback
1. Acknowledge quickly and professionally
2. Offer to help directly (DM/email)
3. Don't argue publicly
4. Take actionable feedback to roadmap

### If Low Engagement
1. Cross-post to additional subreddits
2. Reach out to fitness micro-influencers
3. Consider small Reddit ad campaign ($50-100)
4. Post to Product Hunt (if applicable)
