# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people.
- Accept feedback gracefully.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.
They may remove, edit, or reject contributions or comments that violate this
Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

Report issues to `support@local.invalid`. Include links, screenshots, and
context when possible.

All reports will be reviewed and handled in a timely manner.
